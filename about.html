About



Mission


❝We aim to bring to our customers tailored strategues so that they can achieve their goals sustainably and with the finest results❞


We started our project on the basis of a solid objective: to bridge East and West and support our clients to expand their horizons both geographically and in terms of growth. With our peculiar and multi-cultural roots, we provide high-quality services we can be proud of.




Vision



Our vision is to become the connectors and enablers of our clients dreams. We want customers to feel empowered to aim big knowing that they have strong professionals to support their steps into new lands and new sectors. We are particularly interested in helping brands and companies into new markets and help them bridge cultural and structural market differences to succeed in growing sustainably.




Our Value



We work following our values: environmental and social sustainability, respect and support of minorities, support to the development of women entrepreneurship, the aim to achieve gender and diversity balance in business and in life.






Our story

Marta and Rossella started the business in 2020 to follow their need of creating their own company based on their experience and values. They follow their intuition and passion and that is how Eudunes came to be a bridge between East and West, Europe, and Asia. Our co-founders put their whole expertise and passion into every project and want to progress with their clients with professionalism and deep understanding.
